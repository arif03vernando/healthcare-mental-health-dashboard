# Healthcare Workforce Mental Health Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing the mental health status, job satisfaction, and turnover intentions of 5,000 healthcare employees. The dashboard translates complex survey data into actionable organizational insights to help healthcare institutions address burnout and improve staff retention.

## 📊 Key Metrics & Insights Covered
* **Total Dataset:** 5,000 healthcare employee records.
* **Average Stress Level:** 7.33 / 10, indicating a high-stress environment across departments.
* **Average Job Satisfaction:** 2.2 / 5, highlighting a critical need for workplace culture intervention.
* **Turnover Intention:** 67% of surveyed employees express an intent to leave their current roles.
* **Primary Stressor:** "Heavy Workload" was identified as the leading workplace factor contributing to burnout, significantly outranking career stagnation or safety concerns.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Data Visualization:** Dashboard Design & Formatting
* **Data Analytics:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Descriptive Statistics
* **Core Logic:** Data Segmentation by Employee Type and Department

## 💡 Key Recommendations
1. **Load Balancing:** Redesign shift allocations to mitigate the impact of heavy workloads on Critical Care Nurses and Administrators who show the lowest satisfaction scores.
2. **EAP Optimization:** Although 72% of employees have access to Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs), the high stress level suggests a need to improve the program's utilization rate and trust.
